Get up and running with Pager quickly. This guide will take you from zero to a fully functional team communication setup in just a few minutes.

## Step 1: Create Your Account

Visit [app.pager.team](https://app.pager.team) and sign up for your free account.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up">
    Click "Sign Up" and enter your email address and password, or use Google OAuth for quick registration.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Verify Email">
    Check your email and click the verification link to activate your account.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Create Workspace">
    Choose a name for your team workspace. This will be your team's central hub for communication.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 2: Set Up Your First Workspace

Once logged in, you'll be guided through the workspace setup process.

### Workspace Configuration

<CodeGroup>
```json Example Workspace
{
  "name": "My Team",
  "description": "Our awesome team workspace",
  "domain": "myteam.pager.team"
}
```
</CodeGroup>

<Note>
  Workspace names should be descriptive and easy for your team members to identify.
</Note>

## Step 3: Invite Team Members

Add your team members to start collaborating.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Settings">
    Click on your workspace name and select "Settings" from the dropdown menu.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Go to Members">
    In the settings sidebar, click on "Members" to access user management.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Send Invitations">
    Enter email addresses and click "Invite Members". Your team will receive email invitations to join.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 4: Create Your First Channel

Organize your team communication with channels.

### Channel Types

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Public Channel">
    **Public channels** are visible to all workspace members and perfect for:
    - General announcements
    - Team-wide discussions  
    - Project coordination
    - Company updates
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Private Channel">
    **Private channels** are invitation-only and ideal for:
    - Sensitive discussions
    - Department-specific topics
    - Executive meetings
    - HR communications
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### Creating a Channel

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click the Plus Icon">
    Look for the "+" icon next to "Channels" in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Configure Channel">
    - **Name**: Use lowercase with hyphens (e.g., "product-updates")
    - **Description**: Brief explanation of the channel's purpose
    - **Privacy**: Choose public or private
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Add Members">
    For private channels, select which team members should have access.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 5: Send Your First Message

Start communicating with your team!

### Rich Text Features

Pager supports rich text formatting for enhanced communication:

- **Bold text** with `**bold**`
- *Italic text* with `*italic*`
- `Code snippets` with backticks
- [Links](https://pager.team) with `[text](url)`
- @mentions to notify team members
- File attachments via drag and drop

## Step 6: Set Up Your First AI Agent

One of Pager's most powerful features is AI agents that can help your team.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to AI Agents">
    Click on "AI Agents" in the main navigation or settings menu.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Create Agent">
    Click "Create Agent" and configure:
    - **Name**: e.g., "Team Assistant"  
    - **Description**: What the agent will help with
    - **Instructions**: How the agent should behave
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Test Your Agent">
    Start a conversation with your AI agent by mentioning it in any channel: `@Team Assistant help me find yesterday's meeting notes`
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  AI agents can search through your message history, provide summaries, and help with various tasks. The more you use them, the more helpful they become!
</Tip>
